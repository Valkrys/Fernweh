Your Trips

All of your future and past trips are assembled in one place on your home screen. Your trips are sorted by date and shown with the name you gave it. Click on any trip to access their individual info pages and to get to your travel/plan tickets.

Adding a new Trip

To add a new trip, click on the plus button at the bottom right which will take you to the trip-creation form:

Tips to fill out the Form

! All fields are mandatory !

Name: Can be anything from "honeymoon" to "Spring Vacation", or best the destination you are going to like "Rome" or "Belfast".

Country: This should simply be the name of the country you are going to, it will enable the customs and laws section.

Start & End Date These are simply your arrival and departure dates.

Click submit and you will be redirected to the main trips page, where it will be displayed with your other trips.

Deleting a Trip

To delete a trip, hold and slide the trip card either to the left or right. It will delete it from your list and will not appear in the archived tab.

Editing Trip information

To edit a trip, you go to your trip screen and click on the edit icon on the top right. This will lead you to an editing page where you can edit all the information you previously entered.

Trip information

Here you can choose to look at your trips' individual Travel, Plans and Customs and Laws information or access google maps.

Travel

The travel page is for all of your travel information and tickets. Whether you are flying, taking the train or a coach, you can add all transport information here.
The same goes for your accommodation, whether it is a hotel or an Airbnb.

Clicking on individual travel cards will show you all the saved travel information and gives you easy access to your ticket. If you entered the address or a recognizable name of your destination, clicking on it will take you to it in google maps. You can delete the travel cards by swiping left.

Plans

The plans page is to keep all of your daily plans in one place. Things you might want to add: A restaurant booking, a day tour, a concert ticket. Simply, anything that you are planning on doing and especially any plans that need a pdf ticket for entry.
You can delete the plans cards by swiping left.

Clicking on individual plan cards will show you its saved information and gives you easy access to your ticket. If you entered the address or a recognizable name of your destination, clicking on it will take you to it in google maps.

If you want to add new plan, click on the bottom left plus button in light pink. It will take you to the plan creation form which lets you enter all of its details and add the ticket pdf.

After clicking on the "Add Pdf button" you will be asked to choose a pdf document out of your download folder.

You will know whether the pdf has been added to the form when its name appears next to the button.

After clicking submit, the plan will be added to the list of all plans on the Plans page.

Customs and Laws

This page will give you the relevant travel information you need in order to have a safe and respectful stay in the country of your destination;. The information is coming from the gov.uk website and is regularly updated.

Maps

This will open google maps as a seperate activity and take you straight to the country you are going to for further exploration and trip-planning.
